RS-28 Sarmat

The RS-28 Sarmat is a three-stage Russian silo-based, liquid-fueled intercontinental ballistic missile (ICBM) intended to replace the Soviet R-36M ICBM. It has the world's longest range and is considered one of the most powerful existing ICBM systems.

Key Parameters:

  • Type: ICBM
  • Range: Over 18,000 kilometers
  • Warheads: Multiple nuclear warheads
  • Status: Entered operational service in September 2023

LGM-30 Minuteman III

The LGM-30 Minuteman III is a land-based ICBM in service with the Air Force Global Strike Command and represents the land leg of the U.S. nuclear triad.

Key Parameters:

  • Type: ICBM
  • Range: Approximately 13,000 kilometers
  • Warheads: Multiple warheads
  • Status: Currently operational

Agni-V

The Agni-V is an intercontinental ballistic missile (ICBM) with a range of over 7,000 km, known for its speed and mobility.

Key Parameters:

  • Type: ICBM
  • Range: Over 7,000 kilometers
  • Speed: Up to 29,400 km/h
  • Warheads: Multiple warheads
